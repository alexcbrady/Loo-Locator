About us

At Loo Locator, we believe that comfort and cleanliness shouldn’t be a gamble. Our mission is to help students navigate campus life with ease by providing a platform to discover the best-rated bathrooms around. Whether you’re in a rush between classes or just looking for a comfortable place to freshen up, Loo Locator is your go-to guide for bathroom quality, cleanliness, and accessibility—rated by fellow students who’ve been there. Say goodbye to unpleasant surprises and hello to better bathroom breaks, because your comfort is our priority.
